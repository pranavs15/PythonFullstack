Your company wants a Flask-based Employee Portal where users have different access based on their roles: Admin, Manager, and Employee. Implement role-based access control so that each user can only perform actions allowed by their role.
Requirements:
Roles & Permissions:
Admin: Can create, edit, and delete any employee. Can assign roles.
Manager: Can view all employees and edit only their team’s records.
Employee: Can view and edit only their own profile.
Endpoints:
/login – Login page.
/employees – View all employees (Admin & Manager).
/employee/<id> – View employee profile.
/employee/<id>/edit – Edit employee details (restricted by role).
/employee/<id>/delete – Delete employee (Admin only).
Implementation Details:
Use sessions to track logged-in users and their roles.
Implement a decorator to enforce role-based access on routes.
